About Me

As a junior designer, I believe meaningful digital experiences begin with understanding people. I’m passionate about creating intuitive, human-centered interfaces that make users feel supported and empowered.

Having lived in three different countries, I’ve been shaped by diverse cultures and ways of communicating. This global perspective fuels my curiosity and empathy as a designer. I love exploring cultural behaviors and design traditions around the world, then weaving those insights into inclusive, thoughtful digital experiences.
